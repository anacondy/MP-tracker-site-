About MP Tracker

Our Mission

MP Tracker is a transparent, citizen-focused platform designed to help you stay informed about your elected representatives. We believe in empowering citizens with accessible, accurate information about parliamentary activities, legislation, and the performance of Members of Parliament.

Privacy & Security
