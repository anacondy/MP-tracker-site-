Women's Reservation Bill, 2023 LSRS

Passed

Reserves one-third of seats for women in the Lok Sabha and State Legislative Assemblies. Passed unanimously in RS and with 454/456 votes in LS.

Date: 21 September 2023 By: Ministry of Law and Justice
